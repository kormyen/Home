Sss is an iOS theme

2009-09-07

I created this theme using a jail broken iPhone 3GS by ripping existing 'Cydia' (3rd party app store) downloaded themes and manually replacing the image parts/text files from slices of my steampunk theme design.

The theme entirely worked and the four screenshots below are screenshots from the device itself.

I made the white pixel art icons for my chosen quick access apps (2nd screen). They were weirdly positioned tiny icons at the top of a normal sized app icon.

The other nine app icons were created by someone else.
