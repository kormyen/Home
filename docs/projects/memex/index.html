Memex is a bookmarks and notes cataloging and filtering tool.

"Have you tried writing down everything you know about everything?" - Neauoire

I created this while experimenting with Neauoire's human readable database libraries.

Being a 'sever-less' app makes it easy to setup, share and edit.

Memex works with Hundredrabbits Themes. Just drag a theme file onto the page to change the colours.

I use memex as a personal knowledge base or "exocortex"... basically a digital notebook.

An artist friend Phil James uses Memex for his portfolio.
