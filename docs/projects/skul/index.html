Skul is a monument

2015-08-12

One of several medium scale Minecraft things.

This was made on a public but unlisted vanilla multiplayer Minecraft server running on a home PC.

The eyes appear to follow you as you walk past or fly around it due to an illusion with depth, there are no moving parts.

It has a hidden entrance inside it's mouth with an interior space within the skull.
